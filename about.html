Our Story

Leaply was born from a simple observation: the process of applying to universities and scholarships abroad is unnecessarily complex and often discouraging for many deserving students.

Our founders, having experienced these challenges firsthand during their own international education journeys, realized that with the right technology and approach, we could make this process much more accessible and successful for everyone.

Since our launch in 2024, we've helped thousands of students from over 50 countries find their perfect educational matches and secure over $50 million in scholarship funding.
