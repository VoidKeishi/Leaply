Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Leaply cost?

Leaply offers both free and premium services. Basic university and scholarship search is completely free. Our premium services include personalized counseling, application review, and interview preparation.

Do you help with visa applications?

While we don't directly handle visa applications, we provide comprehensive guidance on the visa process and can connect you with certified immigration consultants when needed.

Can you guarantee admission or scholarships?

We cannot guarantee admissions or scholarships as these decisions are made by universities and scholarship committees. However, we significantly improve your chances through expert guidance and personalized strategies.

How accurate is your matching algorithm?

Our AI-powered matching algorithm has a 95% accuracy rate based on student feedback. It considers over 50 factors including academic profile, interests, career goals, and personal preferences.

Do you work with students from all countries?

Yes, we work with students from over 150 countries worldwide. Our platform supports multiple languages and we understand the unique challenges faced by international students.

How long does the application process take?

The timeline varies depending on your target universities and programs. Typically, we recommend starting the process 12-18 months before your intended start date to ensure ample time for preparation.
